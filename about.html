01 — ORIGIN STORY

Why PROHIT CoreTech Exists.

After a decade of building analytics systems inside large organisations, the same problem kept showing up — businesses drowning in data but starving for decisions. Reports were being produced, dashboards were being built, but the actual business questions remained unanswered.

PROHIT CoreTech was built to fix that gap. Not with a large agency overhead, not with generic deliverables — but with a focused studio that thinks like a Six Sigma practitioner: define the problem, measure honestly, find the root cause, fix it, and make sure it stays fixed.

Co-founded by Pratik Sathe and Priyanka Rohit Barge — combining deep data analytics expertise with high-performance software engineering and commercial AI strategy. Together they bring the business-meets-tech foundation that makes every deliverable actually land.

From Power BI semantic models and DAX pipelines to Generative AI products, mobile apps, SQL automation, MIS reporting and full client websites — we build clean, auditable, decision-ready systems. Based in Mumbai. Available across India and remotely.
