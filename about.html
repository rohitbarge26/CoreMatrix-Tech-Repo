01 — WHO WE ARE

A studio that builds, not just reports.

PROHIT CoreTech is a data, web and process-improvement studio built on 11+ years of hands-on analytics across pharma distribution, FMCG syndicated intelligence and e-commerce MIS. We work where business questions meet engineering — semantic models, ETL pipelines, automation and dashboards leaders actually use.

Founded and led by Pratik Sathe — a Mumbai-based Data & Analytics professional with over a decade of experience across pharma, FMCG and e-commerce. He currently works as a Data Analysis Manager and brings deep expertise in Power BI, SQL, Python, Advanced Excel and cloud data tooling, paired with real business and management grounding.

He has previously led a 25+ member analytics team, onboarding 18 FMCG clients for retailer payout, store audit and competition intelligence studies — managing both the people and the data at scale.

We think like Six Sigma practitioners: define the problem, measure honestly, find the root cause, fix it, and make sure it stays fixed. From Tally-integrated portals to financial reporting tools to full analytics builds and client websites — we ship clean, auditable, decision-ready work.

Grounded in IIM Kozhikode management training and a Computer Science engineering base.
